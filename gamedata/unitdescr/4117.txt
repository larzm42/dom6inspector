The unseelie fay are beings of the Dreamwild, but sometimes they enter the world in deep forest where civilization has never reached. The fay are generally capricious, aloof and sometimes even cruel; but the unseelie are outright malicious and hostile to mankind. Just like the seelie, the unseelie form mock courts with queens ruling over vassals, soldiers and servants. But where the seelie rule over lush forests blessed with perpetual summer, the unseelie courts rule over frozen forests and hostile winter woods. Unseelie Archers are alabaster-skinned warriors wreathed in illusions. They don glittering frost-covered vests that tempers in cold lands, and their bows are stringed with beams of starlight. The unseelie are strongly connected to the winter and they are all resistant to cold.
