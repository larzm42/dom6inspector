The Mage Smiths of Ulm developed skills in metalcrafting surpassed by none. When Fort Vedun surrendered the Ulmish Mage Smiths continued to work as weapon smiths and armorers for the Black Order and the descendants of the Ulmish conquerors. However, the contact with the Zemaitian Veduns has taught them magic never used by their predecessors. Most Zemaitian forts have several Vedun Mage Smiths forging armors for the castle defenders.
