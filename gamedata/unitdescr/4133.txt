Gnu observed Spider and was amazed by his ingenuity; Spider had taught his followers a great many skills. But Gnu was not clever and he could not come up with anything to teach his followers. He asked Spider for advice and Spider told him to teach his followers how to ride, just like he had done. But Spider, weaving his threads, intentionally omitted to mention how to use stirrups, and thus the Gnu Clan Cavalry ride into battle wildly swinging their spears rather than charging their enemies with lances. 
Since Gnu and Rhino became friends and shared grazing grounds, their followers have traded with each other and now Gnu Clan warriors use scale armors of Rhino Clan design. 
Like their patron, Gnu Clan warriors are fierce and strong, but they are considered slow-witted by most other clans. Their commanders do not have the strategic skills of the other Clans.
