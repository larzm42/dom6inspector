The caster summons a Fay Prince from the Dreamwild. The fay are beings of the Dreamwild. They mimic the dreams and legends of mankind and form mock courts with queens ruling over vassals, soldiers and servants. In the frozen forests of the world the Fay reflect the cold and hostile nature of the Dreamwild. Unseelie Princes appear as immaculate knights with alabaster-pale skin and silver locks, wreathed in icy winds and illusions. They are mighty Fay beings serving their queens with their magic and unparalleled battle prowess.
