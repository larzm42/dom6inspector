The caster summons a group of soldiers from the Dreamwild. The Fay mimic the dreams and legends of mankind and form mock courts with queens ruling over vassals, soldiers and servants. In the frozen forests of the world the Fay reflect the cold and hostile nature of the Dreamwild. Unseelie Archers are alabaster-skinned soldiers wreathed in illusions. They don glittering frost-covered vests that tempers in cold lands, and their bows are stringed with beams of starlight. The unseelie are strongly connected to the winter and they are all resistant to cold.
