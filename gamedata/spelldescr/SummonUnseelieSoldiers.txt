The caster summons a group of soldiers from the Dreamwild. The Fay mimic the dreams and legends of mankind and form mock courts with queens ruling over vassals, soldiers and servants. In the frozen forests of the world the Fay reflect the cold and hostile nature of the dreamwild. Unseelie Soldiers are alabaster-skinned warriors wreathed in illusions. They don glittering armors of crystal and ice that tempers in cold lands, and their swords are made of dreamwrought ice.
