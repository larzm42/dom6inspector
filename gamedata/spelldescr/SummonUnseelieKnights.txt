The caster summons a few mounted knights from the Dreamwild. The fay are beings of the Dreamwild. They mimic the dreams and legends of mankind and form mock courts with queens ruling over vassals, soldiers and servants. In the frozen forests of the world the Fay reflect the cold and hostile nature of the Dreamwild. Unseelie Knights wreath themselves in illusions and don glittering armors of crystal and ice that tempers in cold lands, and their swords are made of dreamwrought ice. Unseelie Knights are mounted on Fay horses stronger and swifter than any mount found in human lands. The unseelie are strongly connected to the winter and they are all resistant to cold.
